Statement:

The Commission will establish an information-sharing system for communication between relevant authorities and may grant access to other competent authorities.

Context:

Information sharing system: The Commission is responsible for setting up a secure system for communication between Digital Services Coordinators, the Commission, and the Board, with possible access for other relevant authorities.

Use of system: All communications under this Regulation must use the information sharing system.
