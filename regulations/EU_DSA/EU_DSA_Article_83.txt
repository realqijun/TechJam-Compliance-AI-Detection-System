Statement:

The Commission may adopt implementing acts related to its intervention, including procedures for proceedings, hearings, and information disclosure.

Context:

Implementing acts: The Commission can adopt acts regarding practical arrangements for proceedings (Articles 69 and 72), hearings (Article 79), and negotiated disclosure of information (Article 79).

Public consultation: Before adopting such acts, the Commission must publish a draft and invite comments from interested parties, with a minimum comment period of one month.

Advisory procedure: The implementing acts will be adopted following the advisory procedure set out in Article 88.
