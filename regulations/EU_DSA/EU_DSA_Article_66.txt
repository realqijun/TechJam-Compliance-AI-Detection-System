Statement:

The Commission can initiate proceedings against a provider of a very large online platform or search engine suspected of violating the Regulation, and Digital Services Coordinators must cooperate in the investigation.

Context:

Initiation of proceedings: The Commission may start proceedings under Articles 73 and 74 if a provider is suspected of infringing the Regulation.

Notification: Upon initiating proceedings, the Commission must notify Digital Services Coordinators, the Board, and the concerned provider.

Information sharing: Digital Services Coordinators must provide the Commission with any relevant information about the infringement.

Loss of powers: The initiation of proceedings removes the Digital Services Coordinatorâ€™s enforcement powers under Article 56(4).

Cooperation: Digital Services Coordinators and other competent authorities must assist the Commission in investigations, including using their investigative powers under Article 51(1).
