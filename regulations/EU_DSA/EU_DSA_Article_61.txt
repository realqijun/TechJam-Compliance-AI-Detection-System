Statement:

The European Board for Digital Services is established as an independent advisory group to assist Digital Services Coordinators and the Commission in supervising intermediary services providers.

Context:

Board establishment: The European Board for Digital Services (the Board) is an independent advisory group composed of Digital Services Coordinators.

Advisory role: The Board advises Digital Services Coordinators and the Commission to:

Ensure consistent application of the Regulation and effective cooperation.

Coordinate and contribute to guidelines and analysis on emerging issues within the internal market.

Assist in the supervision of very large online platforms.
