Statement:

The Commission must publish decisions it adopts, including the names of the parties and the main content, while considering the protection of confidential information.

Context:

Publication of decisions: The Commission is required to publish decisions related to interim measures, binding commitments, and penalties, specifying the names of the parties and the key details of the decision.

Protection of confidential information: The publication must respect the rights and legitimate interests of the parties involved, including any confidential information from the provider, other relevant persons, or third parties.
