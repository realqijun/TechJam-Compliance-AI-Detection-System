Statement:

The Board supports coordination of joint investigations, advises on audits, provides opinions and recommendations, and promotes European standards and guidelines for the effective implementation of the Regulation.

Context:

Tasks of the Board: The Board:

Supports joint investigations and assists in the analysis of audit reports for very large online platforms or search engines.

Issues opinions, recommendations, and advice to Digital Services Coordinators, considering the freedom to provide services of intermediary service providers.

Advises the Commission on measures for very large online platforms and search engines, and issues opinions on related matters.

Promotes the development of European standards, guidelines, reports, templates, and codes of conduct, and identifies emerging issues within the scope of the Regulation.
