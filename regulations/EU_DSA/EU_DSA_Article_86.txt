Statement:

Recipients of intermediary services can mandate a body or association to represent their rights under this Regulation, and online platform providers must prioritize processing complaints from such representatives.

Context:

Representation rights: Recipients of intermediary services have the right to authorize a not-for-profit body, organization, or association to represent their rights under this Regulation, provided the organization meets certain conditions, such as operating in accordance with Member State law and having a legitimate interest in ensuring compliance.

Complaint processing: Online platform providers must ensure that complaints submitted by such representatives are processed and decided upon with priority and without unnecessary delays.
