Statement:

Providers must set up a single point of contact so that recipients (users) can communicate directly and quickly with them.

Communication must be:

Electronic,

User-friendly,

Not limited to automated tools (must allow choice of methods).

Providers must publish clear, up-to-date contact information for users to easily find and use.

Context:

Complements obligations under the e-Commerce Directive (2000/31/EC).

Ensures users can reach providers for issues such as complaints, disputes, or clarifications.
