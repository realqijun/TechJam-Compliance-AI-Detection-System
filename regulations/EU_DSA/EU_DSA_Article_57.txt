Statement:

Digital Services Coordinators and the Commission must cooperate and assist each other, particularly by exchanging information and supporting investigations, while ensuring compliance with Union and national laws.

Context:

Cooperation and mutual assistance: Digital Services Coordinators and the Commission must closely cooperate to apply the Regulation efficiently and consistently, including sharing information and notifying each other about investigations and decisions.

Investigation support: The Digital Services Coordinator of establishment can request other Coordinators to provide specific information or use their investigative powers in their Member States. Other competent authorities may also be involved if necessary.

Compliance with requests: The Digital Services Coordinator receiving the request must comply within two months unless the request is insufficiently specified, unjustified, or cannot be fulfilled due to lack of information or legal constraints. A reasoned refusal must be provided if the request cannot be met.
