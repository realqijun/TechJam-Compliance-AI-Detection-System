Statement:

The Commission has five years to enforce penalties, with the period subject to interruptions and suspensions under certain conditions.

Context:

Limitation period: The Commission has five years to enforce penalties, starting from the day the decision becomes final.

Interruption of limitation: The period is interrupted by actions such as modifying the fine, refusing a variation request, or enforcement actions by the Commission or a Member State at the Commission's request.
